## Why south Taiwan (not Taipei)

- Taipei fails the winter gate on SKY, not temperature: northeast monsoon = grey drizzle Dec-Feb.
- The Central Mountain Range blocks that monsoon → Kaohsiung/Tainan winter is a dry sunny season, day 20-24C, mornings 13-15C. Occasional 한파 cold snaps drop days to ~15C (disclosed on page).
- Grass: no dormancy at these temps; February Google-Maps photo of Hsin Yi (in page + imgs/) is the on-page evidence.
